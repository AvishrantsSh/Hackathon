How does it work?
Hospital Management

  • Digital Maintenace of Hospitals Records, such as:
    • Patient Details, minimal data to not endanger privacy
    • Staff Information
    • Availability of facilities, no. of patients in each type of ward, etc.
  • Hassle-free referencing to potent hospitals, due te the avialibility of info pertaining to facilities on the website.
  • Special preferences, and suggestions for vulnerable patients.
Realtime Ambulance System

  • Location Enabled Tracking
  • Tackles Emergency and highly crowding situations seamlessly
  • Information on all regional hospitals, facilities in order to help decide the best place to take the patient in an emergency situation.
  • Identifying the best option for the situation nearby available hospitals based on their occupancy level, and other factors.
Prediction & Analysis

  • Real time patient data with effective clustering so as to identify vulnerable ones and manage them effectively through inter-hospital co-operation by potent hospitals.
  • Assessment of hospital requirements through ML by tracking the consumption of services at a local level - both staff as well as equipment, enabling a better future vision of needs.
  • Research to find patterns in patients to know the disease better and identifying pandemic prone situations quickly.
